You are given a number N, how many ways can we write it as a sum of consecutive positive integers?

Input Description:
N an integer (1<N<1000)

Output Description:
how many ways can we write it as a sum of consecutive positive integers

Sample Input:
9

Sample Output:
3

explanation:
the consecutive number of sums to get N is,
2+3+4 , 4+5 , 9

Sample Output:
2

Sample Output:

Sample Output:

Sample Output:
